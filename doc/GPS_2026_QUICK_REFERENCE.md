## üî¥ Critical Changes - Must Know

### 1. No Automatic Renewal
‚ùå **Must submit application even to maintain current position**
- Previous enrollment does NOT carry over
- "Permanence" requires new application
- Failure to apply = complete exclusion

### 2. 24 CFU No Longer Valid
‚ùå **Old qualification requirement eliminated**
‚úÖ **New: 60 CFU pathways for some categories**
- Check your specific category requirements
- Plan for new qualification if needed
- Existing service still counts

### 3. IT Certification Restrictions
‚ùå **Only Accredia-accredited entities valid**
‚úÖ **Grandfathered: previously submitted certs**
- Verify your current certifications
- May need to re-certify
- Check entity accreditation online

### 4. Re-Entry Algorithm
‚úÖ **NEW: Automatic reconsideration in later phases**
- More meritocratic than before
- Respects expressed preferences
- Higher scores have more value

## üÜò Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ‚ùå **Waiting until last day** to submit
   - Portal may crash under load
   - No time to fix errors
   - Risk missing deadline

2. ‚ùå **Not verifying certification validity**
   - May lose points unexpectedly
   - Too late to fix after submission

3. ‚ùå **Assuming automatic renewal**
   - Must actively apply
   - Even for permanence

4. ‚ùå **Not keeping backup documentation**
   - Need proof if disputes arise
   - Portal issues may require resubmission

5. ‚ùå **Ignoring new requirements**
   - 60 CFU pathways for some
   - Different rules per category
   - Province transfer restrictions
